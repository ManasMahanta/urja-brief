## The Big Signal

**What happened:** In this sample week, the count of plants flagged "critical"
in the CEA daily coal report rose (illustratively) ahead of a heatwave
forecast.

**Why it matters:** Coal stock days measure inventory against expected burn, so
the flag moves with demand forecasts as much as with mining or railway supply.
A rising critical count is a claim about the next few weeks, not the next few
hours — the useful move is to check whether generation at those plants actually
fell in the following daily reports.
