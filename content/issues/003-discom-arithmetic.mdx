## The Big Signal

**What happened:** A sample state's (illustrative) AT&C loss figure improved in
the annual ratings, while its payable days to generators lengthened.

**Why it matters:** Those two numbers pull in opposite directions, and both are
real. DISCOM finances sit between fixed retail tariffs and market procurement
costs, which is why every generation story — renewable PPAs, coal plant
utilisation, exchange volumes — eventually routes through them. Ratings
seasons is when the arithmetic becomes public; the number to quote is the loss
percentage, not the grade.
