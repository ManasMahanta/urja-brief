## The Big Signal

**What happened:** In this sample week, the afternoon renewable share touched a
new (illustrative) high while the evening peak still cleared almost entirely on
coal and hydro.

**Why it matters:** Headlines will report the record share; the system question
is what carries the peak two hours after sunset. Storage tenders, pumped hydro
timelines, and evening-shifted tariffs all exist because of this one shape in
the demand curve. When you read a "renewables overtake coal" story, check the
hour it describes.
